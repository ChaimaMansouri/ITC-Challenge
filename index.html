ENTREPRENEURS AND THE WORK OF TOMORROW

Societal Challenges at the Heart of Future Entrepreneurship The entrepreneur of tomorrow is no longer just someone who pursues their project; they must now, more than ever, grapple with societal challenges and environmental concerns. Giving purpose to their activities is essential. The future entrepreneur doesn’t merely ask what product or service to create; they also focus on solving significant societal issues, such as education or mobility. Humanistic Values Without idealizing the entrepreneur of the future, they will undoubtedly be more connected and globally aware. They will need to consider how to reintegrate societal impacts at both the environmental and social levels to create new business models. These future entrepreneurs will operate in a healthier and more respectful environment.
